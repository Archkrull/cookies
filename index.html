What are cookies?

Here we will learn about different kinds of cookies

Edible cookies

In this article, we don't really care about edible cookies.

Internet cookies

Internet cookies have many different names. "Web cookies", "HTTP cookies", "Browser cookies". When you enter a web site, the site will give you a cookie. This cookie will be saved in your browser. This means that the next time you visit this particular website, you bring your cookie with you and the information entered in that specific cookie, will be remembered.

For example, if you have entered login information in the cookie, that information is remembered and you will not have to enter it a second time. If you don't want your information to be remembered or if you just hate cookies with a seething passion, you can delete them.
